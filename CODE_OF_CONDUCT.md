# Code of Conduct

We are committed to fostering a welcoming, harassment-free community. All participants agree to:

- Be respectful and inclusive; value diverse perspectives.
- Give and receive constructive feedback kindly.
- Assume good intent while addressing impact.
- Use professional and considerate language.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discrimination, personal attacks, and sustained disruption.

## Reporting
If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it to the maintainers at `conduct@foodspec.org`. Reports will be reviewed promptly and handled confidentially.

## Enforcement
Maintainers may take any action they deem appropriate, including warnings, temporary bans, or permanent removal from the community spaces.
